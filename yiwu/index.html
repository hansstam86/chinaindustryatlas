Quick answer

Yiwu (义乌), in Zhejiang province, is home to the Yiwu International Trade City — the world's largest wholesale market for small commodities, with 75,000+ stalls across 6 districts carrying over 2.1 million products. It is the primary global source for low-cost everyday goods.
